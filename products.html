  • Skid mountable to most distributor-built dispensing skid systems
  • Island mountable for multi-dispenser fuel islands (with or without pit)

  • Compatible with third-party fuel management pedestals

  • Optional 2" receipt printer (3" roll)
  • Optional integrated motor-starter with overload protection
  • Optional upgrade to P4-ParaFUEL fuel management system
