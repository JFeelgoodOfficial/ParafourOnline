Common Questions

Before You Call

Here are the most common questions we get from new customers — but don't hesitate to call if you need something more specific.

Are your dispensers legal for retail use in my state?

Yes. Parafour dispensers are NTEP Certified per NCWM Handbook 44, making them legal for retail fuel sales in all 50 US states. We are the only ETL Listed, NTEP Certified Autogas dispenser manufacturer in America.

What meter types are compatible with P4 dispensers?

Our dispensers are compatible with LiquaTech LPM102, Neptune 4DMD, Liquid Controls MA4, LiquaTech LPM-101, and Red-Seal / Micro Motion / Endress Hauser mass flow meters. Contact us for full compatibility details.

Can I integrate my existing fleet card or POS system?

Yes. All P4-series dispensers are pre-wired for connection to most major card reader and POS systems. Our optional P4-ParaFUEL system provides additional fuel management capabilities including transaction reporting and driver ID.

Do you offer on-site installation or technical support?

We provide comprehensive installation documentation and phone / email technical support at no charge. On-site visits are available — contact us to discuss your project location and requirements.

What is the lead time on a new dispenser order?

Lead times vary based on current inventory and configuration options. Contact our sales team for current lead time estimates and to discuss your delivery requirements.

Are your dispensers approved for use in Canada?

Yes. Our ETL Listing covers both US and Canadian compliance (UL 495/1203/1238/913), making Parafour dispensers suitable for Canadian installations. Contact us for country-specific documentation.
