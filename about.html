Our Mission

Built to Be the Best.
Not Just the First.

Parafour Innovations, LLC is dedicated to innovation, technology, quality, and flexibility in LPG dispensing systems. While a newer company by name, Parafour has more than 50 years of experience in the LPG fueling and Autogas industry behind it.

With experience gained at companies like Petrolane, IMPCO, BRC, CleanFUEL USA, and GE — our management and engineering team has designed, built, and serviced thousands of Autogas refueling stations worldwide.

Our goal is to design, manufacture, and support the very best products with advanced technologies, unsurpassed quality, and diverse flexibility in application — products that are LEGAL and APPROVED for use in any application or jurisdiction, nationwide.
